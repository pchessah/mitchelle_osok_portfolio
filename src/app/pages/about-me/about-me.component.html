About Me

I am a policy specialist and a student of politics. Officially my job is to make policies that are centered around human development. But really, my interest lies in the change that strategic policy can impact on society, empowering people and improving their well being whether it is in government, business operations or international development. Therefore, yes, I belong in all rooms but you will find me in the room I believe I am most impactful. Wherever I am, my priority will always be the good of the people. After 7 years working in a legislature, I transitioned to the corporate sector focusing on building relationships with governments and other institutions to spearhead public policy programs that promote public welfare and community engagement. Wherever I am, my priority will always be the good of the people.



Some say politics is in my blood but it doesn’t hurt to be in a classroom debating about political affairs with open minded, extremely intelligent individuals and professors. The freedom to articulate my thoughts and beliefs both verbally and on paper and be positively challenged on them excites me. I love defending my ideas. In a way it’s made me fearless to stand for what I believe in but also humbled me enough to admit there is always room for improvement. I have always found peace and belonging in the classroom and it feels great to be back as a student and I intend to stay on as a professor in the future. But at the end of the day aren’t we all perpetual students of life? We never stop learning. Leadership and learning are indispensable to each other - JFK. I believe even in heaven we will be learning at the feet of the Omniscient.



All in all, I am an Aquarius and my passions are a reflection of this. Like air, I lack a distinct form and often resist classification. I believe I can do anything I set my mind and heart to. I embrace my creativity and individuality and appreciate fun and innovative environments. I am a forward thinker, a risk taker and my actions are always guided by my desire to make the world a better place. I thrive in situations where I can be creative and inventive but can also assist, support and elevate others. There’s enough cake for everyone so why not let others have their slice? And if it runs out we can always bake another cake. I subscribe to the philosophy of compassionate leadership where leaders embrace a culture of kindness and service promoting equity and inclusion. That’s the type of leader I strive to be every single day. Your legacy lies in the lives you touched for better or for worse. If it’s to be it’s up to me! William H. Johnsen.



Finally, I don’t cook yet I have the appetite of a manual labourer. Remember to ask me how that is working out for me when and if we ever meet face to face.
